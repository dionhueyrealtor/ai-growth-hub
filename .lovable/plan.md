

## AI Employee Factory LLC — Landing Page

### Overview
A high-converting, mobile-first landing page for an AI automation agency targeting local service businesses. The design follows a clean, modern SaaS aesthetic with bold typography, layered shadows, and strong CTAs focused on booking appointments.

### Sections (Top to Bottom)

1. **Sticky Navigation** — Blurred glass-effect top bar with logo left, "Book Appointment" CTA button right. Compact `h-16`.

2. **Hero Section** — Two-column layout (stacks on mobile). Left: bold headline ("Your AI Sales Employee That Never Sleeps."), subheadline, 3 icon bullet points, gradient-shadow CTA button. Right: animated "Status Feed" showing live-style cards (incoming call → lead qualified → appointment booked) to make the AI tangible.

3. **How It Works** — 3-card grid (numbered steps): Clone Your Best Salesperson, Automate Your Follow-Up, Optimize for More Appointments. White cards with layered shadows, hover lift effect.

4. **Who We Help** — Section intro + grid of industry cards (Med Spas, Dental, Chiro, Home Services, Salons, Coaches) with icons. Hover reveals a "before/after" metric.

5. **Why This Works** — Grid layout with icon + text pairs. Headline: "Most businesses don't have a lead problem—they have a follow-up problem." Five benefit bullets with modern stroke icons.

6. **Offer Section** — Dark (`bg-zinc-950`) contrast block. Headline: "Get Your AI Sales System Installed in 14 Days." Included items list, risk reversal guarantee in a bordered card, white CTA button.

7. **Footer** — Clean layout with company info (address, phone, email) and policy links.

### Design System
- **Colors**: Zinc 950 primary, Indigo→Purple gradient accent, white surfaces, Zinc 500/600 body text
- **Typography**: Geist Sans / Inter, `tracking-tighter` bold headings, relaxed body
- **Cards**: No borders, layered box-shadows, 16px outer radius
- **CTAs**: Dark solid buttons with indigo glow shadow, scale hover/active effects
- **Motion**: 200ms ease transitions, staggered fade-in entrances, card hover lifts
